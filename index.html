Domestic Flights in Australia

This is a dashboard of domestic flights in Australia in June 2019. Designed by Xinyi Wu.

Routes and Passengers of Flights

On the right is a map of Australia. Hover on a departure city and the routes will emerge. The symbol size in this map is based on the number of passengers.

Number of Trips, Loading factor and Passengers

This bubble plot looks closely at the data of domestic flights in June, 2019. Load factor measures the percentage of available seating capacity that has been filled with passengers. A high load factor is preferred over a low load factor.

Comparison of Domestic Flights in 2019 and 2020

In 2020, the lockdown in Australia took a heavy toll on the airline industry. As can be seen from the chart, the passengers of domestic flights decreased until April.
